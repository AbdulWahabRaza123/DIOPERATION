Enter the following commands to get the result:

  • binarization: to convert image into black and white
  • grey scale: to convert image into gray scale
  • blur: to blur the whole image
  • detect coins:to detect the circles from the image
  • emoboss filter: to enhance edges in the direction of the selected convolution mask(s)
  • clear coins: to make coin holes clear
  • sharp filter: to sharp the image
  • specia filter: to provides the best clarity water for drinking water, coffee machines and tea
  • low brightness: to decrease the brightness of pixel with 5 value
  • increase brightness to increase the brightness of the filter for 5 values
  • low contrast: to make contrast low
  • increase contrast: to increase the contrast
  • gamma correction:to enhance the image
  • inverse transform: to make colors inverse from image
  • log transform: to enhance the image
